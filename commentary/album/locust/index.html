Dutvutan Listen on: YouTube

Bamsy: wiki editor

Zerahypt Planet Entry:

Dutvutan is a primordial Kabrian planet that has been inhabited ever since the dawn of the Kabrian Omnire. It is a bustling world full of powerful entities such as ESEs, Cytonians, and Thilians. Most of the communities there are living in a delicate balance between war and peace.

Its entire history of turmoil has made the dutvutanian civilization one of the most resilient in Kabrius. Every structure and vehicle is built to be ready for almost any physical abuse.

Recently, the planet has been liberated by Dutvutan, a Turgkin who bears the same name as the planet. His intolerance for incompetence has instilled dignity and pragmatism for its people, making it less hostile overall.

Interceptor Listen on: YouTube

Bamsy: wiki editor

Archivist's Note: Despite its name, this vehicle is not quite what you'd expect. It is a dutvutanian vessel used to quickly eradicate local disturbances, with a massive cannon. It is called the Targis-i PP TRI-C.

Syrsa in a comment on the YouTube link:

Ah yes, the Interceptor; -Long Range Penetrator- "Targnilian Rapid Ion - Cannon".

A Targnilian Rapid Ion is a type of matter that can travel faster than the speed of sound through atmosphere without being hindered by the atmospheric impact, literally cutting through air like a hot knife through butter in order to reach its target faster than bullets.

Btw, the reason why it says PP twice is that this is the second strongest in the line of LRP's.
