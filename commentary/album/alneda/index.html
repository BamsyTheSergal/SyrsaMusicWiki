Syrsa ✑ Artist Commentary

When the Thilians were first created, Cyrathilians had yet to be made.

The Thilian sanctum is where all of the new Thilian species originate.

Tyg, their creator, made the Proto-Cyrathilians who are the ancestors of all thilians.

The Thilian Sanctum is where most new Thilians get born but also rest with their ancestors once they perish.
