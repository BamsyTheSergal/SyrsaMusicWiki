Kybrothesian Listen on: YouTube

Bamsy: wiki editor

Archivist's Note: This is Jack, a character that was once a mascot of the channel. He's a Dutvutanian Kybrothesian. He's an intergalactic player, and voluntarily infertile, as he does not want to bring children into the world without first finding true love. He may seem selfish to outsiders, but he truly cares for the lovers he meets, and hopes to one day find the right one.

The Slavuics Listen on: YouTube

Bamsy: wiki editor

Archivist's Note: If one of these trucks passes you by on Terrahypt, you might be able to hear a song like this coming from their radio. The Slauvics were originally founded by Slavic humans who migrated to terrahypt long ago. Nowadays, they employ many Plastmen (artifical humans) to aid with their creation of vodka and other slavic products.

Daunter Listen on: YouTube

Bamsy: wiki editor

Archivist's Note: A sort of "remaster" of Yonk. The art depicts John "Yonk" Kontsley's second vehicle, the Daunter. Its a fast interception craft with a single powerful beam weapon, perfect for quick surgical strikes. Also, his face here is part of the channel Icon.

Yonk Listen on: YouTube

Bamsy: wiki editor

Yonk (Full name John Kontsley) is an Irish Human who was taken to Terrahypt around the time of the first world war. He has since become a agent within the field of "Surgical Offensive Covert Operations", and is a brash trickster, in battle and as a person. His human perseverance is one of the main reason's he's able to go toe to toe with some of the more dangerous persons and vehicles. I personally Love Yonk, as you may or may not have noticed.
